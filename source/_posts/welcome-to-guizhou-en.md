## 🌍 Welcome to Guizhou Travel

Welcome to **Guizhou Qianxing** — a travel blog dedicated to promoting Guizhou province!

### 📖 What You'll Find Here

Guizhou is home to **6 prefecture-level cities, 3 autonomous prefectures, and 88 county-level divisions**. Tucked among mountains and rivers, countless hidden gems await discovery.

Each post covers a different county or city:

- 🏔️ **Natural Scenery**: Waterfalls, caves, karst peaks...
- 🏘️ **Ethnic Culture**: Miao, Dong, Bouyei and other minority traditions
- 🍜 **Local Cuisine**: Sour soup fish, Changwang noodles, beef rice noodles...
- 🗺️ **Travel Tips**: How to get there, where to stay, what to do

### 🔍 How to Navigate

- **Nav bar** → Browse by category or tag
- **Search box** → Find places or dishes instantly
- **Archives** → Browse all posts chronologically
